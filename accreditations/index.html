OZEV Approved Installer

Office for Zero Emission Vehicles is the UK government body governing EV grants. Only OZEV-authorised installers can process the EV Chargepoint Grant for renters and the Workplace Charging Scheme.

What it means for you: we can apply for and apply your grant directly - no separate paperwork.

NICEIC Approved Contractor

The National Inspection Council for Electrical Installation Contracting is the UK's leading electrical certification body. NICEIC contractors are independently assessed against BS 7671 standards.

What it means for you: every install certified compliant, Part P notified, and protected under the NICEIC Platinum Promise.

NAPIT Registered

National Association of Professional Inspectors and Testers - a Government-approved Competent Person Scheme operator. NAPIT registration covers electrical, plumbing, microgeneration and ventilation.

What it means for you: our work is self-certifiable to local building control - no extra inspection fees.

TrustMark

TrustMark is the only Government-Endorsed Quality Scheme covering work in or around the home. Members are vetted on technical competence, customer service and trading practices.

What it means for you: independent dispute resolution and consumer financial protection.

Plug In Stations is the UK's #1 Ohme installer - the only smart EV charger built specifically for UK smart tariffs. Approved partners receive priority hardware, training and warranty support.

What it means for you: first-line support direct from us - not a 3rd-party call centre.
