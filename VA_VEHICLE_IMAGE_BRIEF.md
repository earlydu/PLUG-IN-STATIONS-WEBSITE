# Vehicle Lifestyle Image Brief

## What I need

One **lifestyle photo** per car listed below. Lifestyle = the car in a real-world setting (driveway, urban street, scenic road, customer home) — **not** a studio shot, brochure render, or interior-only.

## Spec

- **Format:** JPG
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:10 (landscape)
- **Min size:** 1600 × 1000 px
- **Quality:** sharp, well-lit, recognisable as the named trim where possible. Front-3/4 angle preferred.
- **Source:** royalty-free or manufacturer press photos. Avoid watermarked stock.

## Where to put them

Save into the existing folder: `vehicle_images/{brand-slug}/{model-slug}.jpg`

Both the brand-slug and model-slug must match exactly the slugs in the table below — they are the same as the URL paths on the live site, e.g. an Audi Q4 e-tron lives at `vehicle_images/audi/q4-e-tron.jpg`.

## Workflow

1. Tick the checkbox below as each image is saved.
2. If the exact trim image cannot be found, use a generic photo of the same model and add a note.
3. If the model has been discontinued or you cannot find anything suitable, leave the box unchecked and add a note.
