Volvo EX90 charging FAQs

What is the range of the Volvo EX90?
The Volvo EX90 has a WLTP-certified range of 374 mi. WLTP is a standardised test all EVs go through in Europe — real-world range varies with driving style, weather and temperature.
What is the battery size of the Volvo EX90?
The Volvo EX90 has a 111 kWh battery. The bigger the battery, the more range you get — and the more flexibility you have to charge during cheap off-peak windows.
What charging connector does the Volvo EX90 use?
The Volvo EX90 uses a Type 2 connector for home and public AC charging, and a CCS connector for rapid DC charging on the go. Every smart EV charger we install supports Type 2.
How fast can I charge the Volvo EX90 at home?
The Volvo EX90 accepts up to 11 kW AC at home. With a 7.4 kW Ohme Home Pro on a single-phase supply, a full 0–100% charge takes around 15h. Most drivers schedule overnight off-peak to wake up full.
What is the rapid-charging speed of the Volvo EX90?
On a public DC rapid charger, the Volvo EX90 can pull up to 250 kW, giving roughly a 20→80% charge in 16m. Useful on long trips — at home, AC charging is cheaper and easier on the battery.
Does the Volvo EX90 qualify for the OZEV grant?
If you rent, own a flat, or run a business you may qualify for up to £350 off via the OZEV EV Chargepoint Grant. Owner-occupier homeowners no longer qualify. Check your eligibility.
Does the Volvo EX90 support bi-directional charging?
Bi-directional (V2H / V2G) charging support varies by vehicle and software version. For a confirmed answer for your specific Volvo EX90 year and trim, request a quote and our team will check before install.
