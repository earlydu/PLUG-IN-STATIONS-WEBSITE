Part S at a glance

From 15 June 2022, all new residential developments in England must include EV charging infrastructure.

  • New homes with parking1 chargepoint per dwelling, min. 7 kW
  • Major renovations >10 spaces≥1 chargepoint + cable routes for 20% of remaining bays
  • New non-residential≥1 chargepoint per ≥10 bays + cabling for 20%
