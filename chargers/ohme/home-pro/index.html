Why the Ohme Home Pro is our most-installed EV charger

The Home Pro is the unit our engineers reach for first when a customer asks "what should I get?". It does the smart-tariff dance better than any other UK home EV charger - pair it with Octopus Intelligent and you'll be charging at 7p/kWh overnight without thinking about it. Plug in, walk away, the Ohme app handles the rest.

What sets it apart from the ePod (and most rivals) is the built-in LED display on the front of the unit. You can see charge state, current power and any error codes without unlocking your phone. For households with multiple drivers or anyone who likes a glanceable status indicator, it's a small detail that pays off daily.

The 5-metre tethered Type 2 cable means no faffing about with separate cables - and Ohme's cable management is genuinely tidy (the unit has integrated cable hooks). It's a slightly larger footprint than the ePod, but still compact enough for any modern home install.
