Why pick the ePod over the Home Pro?

The ePod is what we install when space matters - flats, narrow garage walls, conservation areas where any visible kit needs to be discreet. It's noticeably smaller than the Home Pro (about 30% less wall space), and the lack of a screen gives it a cleaner, almost invisible look once mounted.

The other reason customers choose the ePod is the untethered cable. If you've got two EVs with different cable preferences, or you want to swap your cable for a longer/shorter one over time, untethered gives you that flexibility. Some customers also prefer the tidier look of a unit without a permanently-attached cable hanging off it.

Critically, the ePod uses the same Ohme smart engine as the Home Pro. That means identical tariff support, identical app, identical Octopus Intelligent integration. You're not giving up any smart features - just the LED display and the tethered cable.
