About the Easee One

Easee is the Norwegian newcomer that's become a UK favourite among installers. The Easee One is genuinely modular - multiple units chain together on a single feed and dynamically load-balance, which is rare at this price point.

For multi-EV households or homes planning a second EV in the next few years, Easee is excellent value: install one now, add another in series later without re-wiring.
