About the Indra Smart Lux

Indra is a British EV charger maker focused on bidirectional and grid-services-ready hardware. The Smart Lux is their consumer model - a sleek untethered unit with proper solar export integration via the Indra Smart Sense.

Unique selling point: Indra is one of the few UK manufacturers actively building toward V2H (vehicle-to-home) and grid services. If you want hardware that'll be relevant in 5-10 years as bidirectional charging matures, this is one of the few smart bets.
