About the Pod Point Solo 3S

Pod Point is the UK's most-installed EV charger brand by total units. The Solo 3S is the latest generation home unit - tethered, 7.4 kW, with a tidy app and class-leading customer support.

It's not the smartest tariff-wise (Ohme edges it for Octopus Intelligent), but it's a known, reliable quantity. Many fleet drivers have a Pod Point at home because their work network uses Pod Point; same app to use both.
