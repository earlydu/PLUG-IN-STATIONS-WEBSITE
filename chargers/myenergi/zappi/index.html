About the MyEnergi Zappi

Zappi is the original solar-first EV charger. If you generate any of your own electricity (solar PV, wind, or home battery), the Zappi pulls priority from your renewables before the grid - meaning your charge sessions can be effectively free.

It has three charging modes: Fast (full grid), Eco (mix of solar/grid), and Eco+ (solar/wind only - uses zero grid power). For homes building out a smart-energy ecosystem (eddi, libbi battery, harvi sensors), Zappi is the natural pick.
