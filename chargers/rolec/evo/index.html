About the Rolec EVO

Rolec is one of Britain's longest-established EV charger manufacturers. The EVO is their consumer-meets-workplace unit - built to commercial standards but sized and priced for home installs.

Where Rolec excels is in tougher environments: outdoor parking, exposed sites, multi-driver households. The IK10 impact rating means it shrugs off bumps and weather that'd crack a lesser unit.
