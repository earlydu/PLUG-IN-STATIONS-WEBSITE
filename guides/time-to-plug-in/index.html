Chapter 03 - Survey

What we check before we lock the price.

A fixed-price quote needs accurate inputs. These are the questions we'll need answers to (most can be filled in from your sofa).

About your home

  • Postcode and on/off-street parking arrangement
  • Distance from your electricity meter to the charger location (we'll confirm with photos)
  • Cable run preference: surface clipped, hidden in trunking, or fished through walls
  • Approximate age of your consumer unit (the one with all the trip-switches)
  • Any solar PV / home battery already installed

About your EV

  • Make & model (and on-board AC charging speed - 7.4 kW or 11 kW)
  • Connector type (every modern UK EV is Type 2 + CCS)
  • Whether you've taken delivery, signed an order, or are leasing

About your tariff

  • Current energy supplier & tariff (we'll suggest a switch if it's worth it)
  • Approximate annual mileage to size your savings
  • Whether you'd like Octopus Intelligent set up at install

Grants & Finance
Chapter 09 - Grants & Finance

Who pays less. And how.

Three ways the install can cost you less. We'll handle the paperwork on all of them.

1. The OZEV EV Chargepoint Grant - up to £350

If you rent, own a flat, or run a business, the OZEV grant covers up to £350 of the install cost. Owner-occupier homeowners no longer qualify (residential grant ended 2022). The grant comes off your invoice up front, not as a refund.

  • Renters - landlord consent required (we provide a template)
  • Flat owners - no extra paperwork beyond the standard claim
  • Businesses - up to £350 per socket, max 40 sockets

2. 0% finance via Bumper

Spread the install cost over 12-36 months at 0% interest. Soft credit check, no deposit, takes a few minutes online. The OZEV grant still applies.

3. Salary sacrifice (BIK savings of 25-50%)

If your employer offers an EV salary-sacrifice scheme, you can lease an EV (and often the install) out of your gross salary. Benefit-in-kind on EVs is around 3% in 2026, vs 25%+ on petrol cars. Worth asking your HR team.

Common Questions
Chapter 11 - FAQ

The questions we get most often.

Short answers below. Longer answers in our online guides at pluginstations.co.uk/guides.

Can I install if I don't have my EV yet?

Yes. Most customers install ahead of their EV arriving. A signed order or lease counts as proof of vehicle for the OZEV grant.

Do I need 3-phase power?

No. Standard UK homes have single-phase, which charges at 7.4 kW - enough to fully charge any EV overnight. 3-phase is only useful for commercial or high-demand installs.

How long does an install take?

3-5 hours for a standard install. Up to a day if a consumer unit upgrade or earth rod is needed.

Can the cable be hidden?

Yes - in trunking, fished through walls, or run along skirting boards. Tell us your preference at quote stage.

Is the charger weather-proof?

Yes. All units we install are IP54 or better - rated for permanent outdoor use in UK weather.

What if I move house?

You can take the charger with you (we can decommission and re-install at the new property for a fixed fee). Most movers leave it as a value-add for the buyer.

Will it trip my main fuse?

Modern smart chargers have dynamic load balancing - they automatically reduce charging speed if the rest of the house is drawing heavy load (oven, kettle, immersion). No, it won't trip.
