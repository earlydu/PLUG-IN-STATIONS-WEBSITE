This policy explains how Plug In Stations Ltd ("we", "us", "our") collects and uses your personal data when you visit our website, request a quote, or have an EV charger installed by us. We follow the UK General Data Protection Regulation ("UK GDPR") and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who we are

Plug In Stations Ltd is a UK-registered EV charger installer. We are the data controller for the personal data described in this policy. Our contact details are at the end.

2. Data we collect

We collect the minimum we need to quote, book and complete an installation, and to run our website.

  • Identity & contact: name, address, email, phone number, postcode.
  • Property & install: photos of your home / parking / consumer unit, electricity supply type, distance from meter to charger location.
  • Vehicle: make, model, on-board AC charging speed.
  • Tariff & energy: current supplier and tariff (only when you ask us to advise on a switch).
  • Order & payment: products purchased, OZEV grant claim details, finance referral details (handled by Bumper, our finance partner).
  • Web analytics: IP address (anonymised), pages visited, device and browser, referring page. We use this to improve the site. See our cookie policy.
  • Customer service: records of calls, emails, WhatsApp and chat messages so we can resolve queries.

We do not collect special-category data (health, religion, biometrics) and we never knowingly collect data from children.

3. How we use your data

  • Producing your fixed-price quote and arranging the survey.
  • Booking and completing your installation, including ordering hardware and certifying the work (NICEIC, DNO).
  • Submitting OZEV grant claims and processing 0% finance applications on your behalf.
  • Providing aftercare during the 5-year workmanship warranty (and 3-year hardware warranty).
  • Sending operational messages: appointment reminders, install ETA, follow-up checks.
  • Sending marketing emails about new products, smart tariffs and grant changes (only if you opt in — and you can unsubscribe any time).
  • Complying with our legal obligations (tax, electrical certification, anti-fraud).
  • Improving the website and our quoting process.

4. Our legal bases

  • Contract: to quote, install and warrant the work you've asked us to do.
  • Legal obligation: safety certification, DNO notification, tax records, OZEV grant administration.
  • Legitimate interests: running the website, fraud prevention, asking for a Trustpilot review after install.
  • Consent: non-essential cookies and marketing emails. You can withdraw consent at any time.

5. Who we share data with

We only share what's needed to deliver your install or run the business:

  • Hardware partners (Ohme, Easee, etc.) — for warranty registration of the unit fitted in your home.
  • OZEV / DNOs — for grant claims and electrical notifications.
  • Bumper — only if you choose 0% finance.
  • Octopus Energy — only if you ask us to set up Octopus Intelligent on install day.
  • HMRC and our accountants — for invoice and tax records.
  • Our hosting / email / analytics providers — under data-processing agreements.

We never sell your personal data, and we never share it with advertisers for retargeting.

6. How long we keep your data

  • Quote enquiries that don't book: 12 months, then deleted.
  • Customer install records: kept for the warranty term plus the legal retention period for electrical certification (currently 7 years).
  • Marketing list entries: kept while you stay subscribed; removed within 30 days of unsubscribing.
  • Web analytics: aggregated and anonymised after 14 months.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify anything that's inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten") where we no longer have a lawful reason to keep it.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Portability — get a copy in a common format you can take elsewhere.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing or non-essential cookies at any time.
  • Complain to the UK regulator, the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk), if you think we've handled your data badly.

To exercise any of these, email us at enquiries@plugin-stations.com. We'll respond within one calendar month.

8. International data transfers

Your data is stored in the UK and the EEA. A handful of our service providers (e.g. analytics, email delivery) may process it in the US under the UK Extension to the EU-US Data Privacy Framework, or under approved Standard Contractual Clauses with additional safeguards.

9. Security

We use TLS for all web traffic, encrypted storage for customer records, role-based access controls for our staff, and we run regular backups. No system is 100% secure, but we work hard to protect your data and we'll notify you and the ICO if a breach materially affects you.

10. Children

Our services are aimed at adults (homeowners, renters, fleet managers). We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you think a child has provided data, please contact us and we'll delete it.

11. Changes to this policy

We'll update this page when we change how we handle data. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision. For material changes we'll also email anyone on our marketing list and post a notice on the home page.
