1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files saved by your browser when you visit a site. They let the site remember things between page loads (your theme choice, whether you're logged in, etc.) and help us understand how the site is used.

We also use closely-related browser-storage technologies: localStorage and sessionStorage. They serve the same purpose and we treat them the same way as cookies for the purposes of this policy.

2. Types of cookie we use

We split cookies into two groups:

  • Essential — needed for the site to work. No consent required under PECR.
  • Optional — analytics and embedded media. We only set these after you opt in via the banner.

5. Third-party embedded content

Some pages embed content from other companies which set their own cookies if you interact with them. We use the privacy-extended ("no-cookie") version where it's available, but if you click play / sign in / submit, the third party may still set cookies under their own privacy policy:

  • YouTube — case-study videos. We use youtube-nocookie.com embeds where possible.
  • Trustpilot — review widget on landing pages.
  • Google Maps — location pages.

6. How to manage cookies

  • Use the Manage cookie preferences button at the top of this page to change your choice at any time.
  • Clear or block cookies in your browser settings — every browser has a "Site settings" or "Privacy" panel.
  • Set your browser to "Do Not Track" — we respect that flag for non-essential cookies.

Blocking essential cookies / localStorage may break parts of the site (e.g. dark mode won't be remembered between visits, the cookie banner will keep appearing).

7. Changes to this policy

We'll update this page when the cookies on the site change. Material changes will trigger a fresh cookie banner so you can re-consent.
