  • Most home installs cost £580–£1,200 fully fitted, including EV charger, labour, certification and any small extras. The Ohme ePod starts from £849 fitted; the Ohme Home Pro from £999. Use our cost calculator for a tailored estimate.
  • From enquiry to installation we typically work to a 7–10 day window. The install itself takes 3–5 hours on a single visit.
  • Renters, leaseholders and flat owners with off-street parking can claim up to £350. Businesses can claim up to £350 per socket (max 40) under the Workplace Charging Scheme. Run our grant checker for an instant answer.
  • The Ohme Home Pro is our top recommendation for most UK homes - smart, tariff-aware, and works flawlessly with Octopus Intelligent. As the UK's #1 Ohme installer we know it inside out. See our full 2026 EV charger review.
  • Yes - we offer 0% finance via Bumper, with a soft credit check that won't affect your rating. Spread the cost over 3 months at no extra charge, decision in seconds.
  • For most homes with off-street parking, no planning permission is needed under permitted development rights. Listed buildings, conservation areas, and on-street installs may need consent - we flag any issues during the survey.
  • A smart EV charger talks to your energy tariff and the grid, scheduling charging during the cheapest off-peak windows. With Octopus Intelligent + an Ohme EV charger, drivers pay as little as 7p/kWh overnight - saving £80–£150 per month vs petrol.
  • Our standard install includes up to 10 metres of cable. Longer runs (up to 20m+) are quoted as a small add-on - typically £80–£300 depending on cable spec and route. Anything past 25m we'll send a free site visit before pricing.
